Graph Types

Our tool supports two types of graphs:

  • Line Graph: Ideal for showing trends over time.
  • Bar Graph: Perfect for comparing quantities across categories.

How to Use

  1. Upload your data file using the "Upload Data File" section.
  2. Customize your graph settings in the "Graph Customization" section.
  3. Click "Apply Settings" to generate your graph.

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported?

Currently, we support CSV files with two columns for X and Y values.

Can I save the generated graph?

Yes, you can right-click on the graph and select "Save Image As..." to save it to your computer.

How do I reset the graph settings?

Simply refresh the page to reset all settings to their default values.
